Selected Projects

EPIC LAB · SJTU

Large-Scale Video Foundation Model Systems (Wan14B)

Built a distributed training framework for a 14B-parameter video generation model on an 8×A800 cluster, resolving OOM bottlenecks with native PyTorch FSDP, gradient checkpointing, and CPU offloading.

LoRA · Distillation · Custom Loss · Checkpoint Resumption

ICLR 2026

FastVMT: Efficient Video Motion Transfer

Identified redundancy in motion transfer for large video models and proposed a tuning-free reduction mechanism based on local windowing and gradient reuse, achieving 3.43× inference speedup.

Motion Transfer · Efficiency · Video Generation

DEPLOYMENT

Efficient Diffusion Deployment

Investigated and deployed efficient inference methods for diffusion pipelines including Stable Diffusion and FLUX, verifying robustness across architectures and achieving major practical speedups with limited quality degradation.

Caching · Deployment · Diffusion Inference
